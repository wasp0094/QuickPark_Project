QuickPark

Why QuickPark?

QuickPark is a smart parking application which is here to help make parking easier within communities while also connecting you to shopping malls, local restraunts and movie theatres. We have built a platform that is the total one stop shop for parking. With the click of the button, you can now instantly book a convinient, safe and legitimate parking spots for your vehicles. With our user-friendly and easy to use map interface, choose from thousands of parking spaces available on an hourly/daily or rolling monthly basis. Receive instant confirmation and parking instructions.
No more parking worries, no more driving around in circles. Turn up and park following your provided instructions for a hassle-free experience.

About Us

Our mission here is simple, to connect with people and help make parking easier. Living in India, we all know about miseries faced by vehicles owners while looking for an optimal parking spot. After much research, our team of four developers from India, came to a conclusion that there was a need for an India specific smart parking application. Henceforth, we came up with the idea of QuickPark.

FAQs

How does QuickPark works?

Search your desired location and check the availabilty with a single click. Confirm your booking and you are good to go. It's that simple!


What happens after I book a space?

We will inform the owner of the space and automatically send you the full space address and any extra details.


Will my booked space be available?

Yes - we only confirm bookings in line with the availability set by the space owner or operator.


Do I need to enter/leave at the times booked?

You can arrive and leave any time within the booked window.
